Solution Overview

Verto Wave's Data Protection & Privacy solution helps you safeguard your most valuable asset—your data—while navigating the complex landscape of privacy regulations. In an era of increasing data breaches and stringent compliance requirements, a robust data protection and privacy program is essential for building trust with your customers and avoiding costly penalties. Our solution provides a comprehensive framework to discover, classify, protect, and govern your sensitive data, wherever it resides. We help you implement a defense-in-depth strategy that combines data loss prevention (DLP), encryption, and privacy-enhancing technologies (PETs) to protect your data from both internal and external threats. With our Data Protection & Privacy solution, you can build a culture of data stewardship, ensure compliance with regulations like GDPR and CCPA, and unlock the value of your data while respecting individual privacy.
