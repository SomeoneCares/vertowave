Solution Overview

Our Data Protection & Privacy solution provides end-to-end data security, privacy compliance, and data loss prevention (DLP) capabilities. We help organizations discover, classify, protect, and govern sensitive data across all environments while ensuring compliance with global privacy regulations (GDPR, CCPA, HIPAA). The solution includes data discovery and classification, encryption, tokenization, DLP policies, and privacy rights management. By integrating with **StackX's** compliance framework and leading security platforms, we deliver comprehensive data protection that balances security requirements with business needs while maintaining regulatory compliance.
